About Us


Our shop krishna bamboo furniture is started from 2000,The creator of it is Mr. Hardam Singh.We are certified by the indian ministry of textle industry and dastkari vikas samiti (regd. under societies act(XXI)-1860). We makes handicraft items and participate in the exhibitions.We have started our business by manufacturing cane furniture and then bamboo furniture.We have accompanied a lot of varities in our furniture.


We are manufacturing and selling the antique furniture which is affordable by everyone.We gives a clear furnishing to our furniture.We also provides help to our customer for the maintenance and repairing work of the proudcts.There is a good lifetime of our furniture.
